Introduction to Chhatrabas

Chhatrabas is a web-based application aimed to provide a hostel room for those students who do not have a clear choice of stay. It is a website that will provide information on numerous hostels, such as their address, gallery, reviews, and the number of rooms that are available in individual hostel.

It is always hard for students to attain information about the various hostels such as, distance of hostels from the university facilities, the available conditions about the hostels, satisfaction with transport, hostel security, room size, and safety. Students face a lot of problems when looking and identifying hostels for residing in. These include; higher prices charged by brokers and some hostels owners, tiresome and time-wasting process of looking for hostels. In addition, hostel owners find it difficult to handle studentsâ€™ and employee information.

With this system in place, we can improve the efficiency of the hostel operations while saving the time of the students, thus overcome the drawbacks of the existing manual system. There will be various criteria for the booking of hostel rooms. If the user finds the type of hostel or hostel room they are looking for on this page, they can reserve it. The influence of the environment and accommodation on the satisfaction level of all students has been a popular area of study and of compelling interest to studies. In relation to this, the good hostel condition has positive influence on the overall student enrolment.

Why Chhatrabas?

We came across few individual hostel websites where the hostel owners have provided information about the services they offer, the hostel fee, and other additional information through which students can reserve the room. These systems were also designed to store the data of the students who is currently residing in the hostel and to verify the personal profile of all the current students within few minutes. The system was also capable to automatically calculate all the bills and issues notifications to those students. Because this approach was limited to a single hostel, students were limited to just selecting one from that particular hostel.

We also discovered a small number of websites that featured hostels, but they just provided basic information about the hostels and their locations, with no option to book through the system. These websites were not much useful as it did not have much features in it (System and System 2021). In comparison to these websites, the system we are developing is somewhat comparable, but it is still quite different because we have planned to provide much more elevated features such as government certified hostels can only register themselves in this system. Similarly, Hostel can announce their vacancy and even update their profile with various information including the price, number of roommates, etc. Since, this is student focused system students are recommended with hostel near their chosen university, college, or school. Students can view the rooms with the different price ranges and can reserve the room through this system only.
